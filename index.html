Welcome to the Stock Intrinsic Value Calculator

This tool is designed to provide an estimate of a company's intrinsic value based on a 10-Year Discounted Free Cash Flow (DFCF) model. It is a web-based replica of the core functionality found in the "KK Prototype Moneycessity" spreadsheet.

Methodology: Discounted Free Cash Flow (DFCF)

The DFCF model is a valuation method used to estimate the value of an investment based on its expected future cash flows. The core idea is that the value of a company is the sum of all its future free cash flows, discounted back to their present value.

  • Free Cash Flow (FCF) Projections: We project the company's FCF for the next 10 years based on user-provided growth rates.
  • Terminal Value: We calculate a terminal value, which represents the company's value for all years beyond the 10-year projection period.
  • Discount Rate: Future cash flows are discounted to their present value using a specified discount rate to account for the time value of money and risk.
  • Intrinsic Value: The sum of the discounted FCF and the discounted terminal value gives us the enterprise value. We then adjust for cash and debt to arrive at the equity value, which is divided by the shares outstanding to find the intrinsic value per share.

How to Use This Calculator

  1. Navigate to the "Data Entry" Tab: All inputs required for the calculation are located on this page.
  2. Enter Company Financials: Fill in the fields for the company's stock ticker, current price, shares outstanding, latest free cash flow, cash, and total debt. All monetary values should be entered in millions.
  3. Provide Your Assumptions: Input your estimates for the company's growth over the next 1-5 years and 6-10 years. You must also provide a terminal growth rate (the perpetual rate you expect the company to grow at after 10 years) and a discount rate.
  4. Calculate & View Results: Once all fields are filled, click the "Calculate & View Dashboard" button. You will automatically be taken to the "Dashboard" tab to see the detailed results.
